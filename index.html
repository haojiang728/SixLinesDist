About

Six Lines is a practical method of I Ching divination that uses six individual lines to form a hexagram. Each line carries meaning, movement, and relationships that reveal the deeper dynamics of a situation.

This method comes from the Wen Wang Gua tradition, a lineage that interprets the I Ching using stems, branches, families, and changing lines to create a detailed picture of timing, influences, and outcomes.

Six Lines is valued for its clarity. Instead of relying only on poetic text, it analyzes the structure of the hexagram itself — the energies, interactions, and transformations happening beneath the surface.

This application serves as a tool that charts Six Lines hexagrams according to the Wen Wang Gua system and provides AI‑assisted interpretations based on the chart’s structure. It offers a precise and accessible way to explore this traditional method without replacing classical study or practice.

How to Cast

1. Focus: Clear your mind and focus intently on your question.

2. Three Coins: Take three identical coins. Toss them together six times.

3. Record: The first toss represents the bottom line (Line 1). Subsequent tosses build the hexagram upwards to Line 6.

4. Values:

  • 3 Tails: Old Yin (Changing)
  • 1 Head, 2 Tails: Young Yang
  • 2 Heads, 1 Tail: Young Yin
  • 3 Heads: Old Yang (Changing)
